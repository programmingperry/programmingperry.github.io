Hey, where's Perry?

Welcome to my space on the internet, where I intend to share my coding learning journey and my projects along the way.

When the topic "Hardware" began in school, I had to ask what exactly peripherals are. While this seems pretty clear in english, as far as I can tell, it isn't that simple in German, since the word has several meanings in geographie, IT, linguistics, biology, philosophy etc.

That's how Perry somehow established and stuck to me. And when I hear Perry, I think of one of my favourite shows from childhood - Phineas and Ferb. Perry is their teal colored Platypus, who apparently doesn't do much but actually has a secret life as a special agent, fighting his evil nemesis Dr. Doofenschmirz.

Since then I've decided to make Perry my kind of emotional support / spirit animal on my way to becoming a programmer.
